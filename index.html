About

I’m a fifth-year Ph.D. student at the University of Southern California’s Institute for Creative Technologies (ICT), specializing in human-agent dispute resolution. My work focuses on AI-mediated dispute resolution and negotiation, preference elicitation, agent design, and embodiment. I design and run large-scale human-subject studies to evaluate these systems.
